Hi! I'm Tyler Berg.

I'm a multidisciplinary designer and educator that lives at the intersection of design and code.

Email Me View Resume

Currently, I’m a senior designer at Brunch Digital, where I lead the visual design production of website and application interface – and craft HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to turn static ideas into interactive reality. The result is organic, effective results for users while accomplishing specific client objectives.

I'm also an adjunct instructor at my alma mater, American University, where I teach the fundamentals of typography.

My background includes experience with clients in both the public and private sector, as well as non-profit institutions and Fortune 500 companies. Prior to working at Brunch Digital, I spent time learning and designing at Friendly Design Co. and Bully Pulpit Interactive.

I love to teach, write, and speak about design. I live and work in Washington, DC.
